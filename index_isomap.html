10-dimensional clustered data reduced to 3D

What it does: Isomap preserves distances along the data manifold - think of it as preserving "walking distance" rather than straight-line distance. Points that are neighbors in high-dimensional space stay neighbors in 3D. This is particularly good for curved or non-linear structures where straight-line distances are misleading. This is an unsupervised, non-linear technique.
